Hello! We are the Mars Club from Keystone academy.
Mars Club's mission is to emigrate humans from Earth to Mars
in order to avoid the disasters from Earth.
Mars Club was founded in 2018 by Leo Deng and Tim Gu.
We thought about this idea to evacuate Earth because
we read a lot articles about disasters on Earth, such as: Climate Change (a big origin of all the natural disasters on earth),
and robots rebellions (A.I having their own feelings, taking over humans)
We started with four people, then more people joined our club.
In 2020 during the pandemic, we talked to Mr. McKenzie,
our head of school about our idea.
after we decided to make a KAP to spread our idea,
we planned to complete our mission in the future with 3 main steps;
Spread, Prepare, and Action.
If you are interested, you can participate in our mission to Mars.
We will be appreciating this a lot.
